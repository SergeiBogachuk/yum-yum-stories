How pricing works

The site now leads with self-load. 10 and 15 yard drop-off rentals are priced near the top of the market because leaving a trailer on-site for several days has to pay for trailer time, delivery, pickup, and disposal risk. Quick load & haul stays available when the customer wants faster service.

FAQ

What customers usually ask before booking.

What sizes are you running right now?

Right now the site is built around 10 yard and 15 yard trailer rentals. That keeps the offer simple, covers the most common cleanup jobs, and makes booking easier.

What is the main service you offer?

The main site pitch is now self-load rental in 10 yard and 15 yard sizes. Quick load and haul is still available when the customer wants faster turnaround instead of keeping the trailer for days.

Why is self-load rental priced so high?

Because a multi-day drop-off ties the trailer up on one job. The rate has to cover trailer time, delivery, pickup, and the disposal risk even before the final dump weight is known.

What if I do not know the exact weight?

That is normal. Use the calculator as a quick estimate, then text a few photos for a tighter quote. The final weight is confirmed after disposal.

How many days are included on self-load rental?

The pricing shown on the site can cover up to 3 days for standard self-load rental. If the trailer needs to stay longer, dispatch can add extra-day pricing.

When do overage charges apply?

Only when the actual dump weight is above the included weight limit for the debris type and size you booked. Standard jobs are shown around 2.0 tons included, and heavier material can be closer to 1.5 tons included. Anything above the limit is billed at $120 per ton.

Can I pay by Zelle?

Yes, Zelle can be your preferred payment method for deposits and balances if your business bank account supports it. Card or invoice payment can still be offered when a customer wants a formal payment link.

Can I just text instead of filling everything out?

Yes. If it is easier, text the address and a few photos to dispatch and you can get the conversation moving that way.
